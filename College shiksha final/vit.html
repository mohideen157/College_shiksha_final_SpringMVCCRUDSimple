VIT University

  • VIT University - Vellore,Chennai

  • Chennai, Tamil Nadu

  • ₹70,000 - ₹215,000

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) was established in 1984 by Dr G. Viswanathan (Minister in Tamil Nadu Govt.) as a self-financing institute named Vellore Engineering College. In 2001, the Vellore Engineering College was granted the University status by the Union Ministry of Human Resources Development. The institute is accredited Grade A by NAAC and is approved by UGC. The main aim of VIT is to impart impeccable technical knowledge to its students so that they become technologically superior.The courses offered at the institute engulf the needs of a wide range of industries like Automotive, Manufacturing, Aerospace, Architecture, Construction, Defence, Energy, Nano Technology, Environment and Transportation.

VIT Vellore Course:

Courses : VIT’s academics is structured around nine schools listed below-
1. School of Bio Sciences & Technology (SBST)
2. School of Information Technology & Engineering (SITE)

4. School of Social Sciences & Languages (SSL)
5. School of Advanced Sciences (SAS)

8.VIT Business School (VITBS)

Accreditation : VIT Universty was Accredited with Grade A & Accredited with Grade A NAAC, India in 2015.

Admission Process & Eligibility :

VIT Vellore grants admission to BTech and BDes programmes through VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE), an entrance exam held by the institute. It is held in April and May every year. Since 2013, it has been organised online. The institute admits students in the BArch programme based on the NATA score. Admissions to rest of the courses are granted on the basis of candidate's performance in the previous qualifying examination.

VIT Vellore BTech admission is based on qualifying VITEEE 2020.

Admission to BDes is based on a valid score in UCEED 2020.

Admission to BArch is done solely based on the score obtained in NATA 2020 followed by counselling rounds.

Placements :

VIT provides students with excellent employment opportunities. It has an exceptional placement record and ensures a high ROI. The institution has made its place in the Limca Book of Records for placing the highest number of students in the IT sector for seven consecutive years. In the placement season of 2018-19, 690 well-reputed companies took part in campus recruitment and made 6180 job offers were made to the students.
Apart from 238 Job offers with CTC 6 LPA by Wipro Technology, the following MNCs extended the highest offers from a single institute (VIT).
