Sathyabama University

  • Sathyabama University,Chennai

  • Chennai, Tamil Nadu

  • ₹20,000 - ₹80,000

Dr Jeppiaar founded the Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology (A Christian Minority Institution) formerly known as Sathyabama Engineering College in 1987. It is a leading institute of architecture, research, technology and education awareness. The Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology (Referred to as University) offers 27 UG-level and 41 programs offersSathyabama Institute of Science and Technology.

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology has been awarded by the Ministry of Human Resources Development (MHRD), Government of India as Category "A" University and accredited by NAAC with Class "A" in 2017, certified with DNV-GL ISO 9001 standard and providing exemplary education from the last 29 years.

On 22 June 2016, The University wrote a special page in the history of space exploration with the launch of "SATHYABAMASAT" in collaboration with ISRO. One example is the launch of "SATHYABAMASAT" and accolades received on world platforms.

Admission Process & Eligibility :

VIT Vellore grants admission to BTech and BDes programmes through VIT Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE), an entrance exam held by the institute. It is held in April and May every year. Since 2013, it has been organised online. The institute admits students in the BArch programme based on the NATA score. Admissions to rest of the courses are granted on the basis of candidate's performance in the previous qualifying examination.

VIT Vellore BTech admission is based on qualifying VITEEE 2020.

Admission to BDes is based on a valid score in UCEED 2020.

Admission to BArch is done solely based on the score obtained in NATA 2020 followed by counselling rounds.
