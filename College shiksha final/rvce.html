Rashtreeya Vidyalaya College of Engineering (RVCE) is a private technical college in Bangalore. The college was established in the year 1963 and is one of the earliest self-financing engineering colleges. It is managed by Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust. The institute is one of the country’s leading engineering institute. The college was ranked 58th in 2018 and 63rd in 2019 by NIRF. RVCE Bangalore offers a student exchange program in collaboration with the University of Applied Science Würzburg-Schweinfurt from Bavaria, Germany. Students who have completed a certification course at RVCE will be eligible to apply to study at FHWS in Mechatronics, Business and Engineering and Logistics in any engineering program. During the course, a six-month internship also has to be done.

RVCE Course:

Courses : RVCE offers a full-time four year BTech programme in 12 specialisations, namely Chemical Engineering, Aerospace Engineering, Biotechnology, and many more. It also offers full-time two year MCA and MTech programme at PG level. MTech is offered in 13 specialistions, including Highway Technology, Information technology, Biomedical Signal Processing & Instrumentation, and many more.
