Vel Tech University

  • Vel Tech University - Chennai

  • Chennai, Tamil Nadu

  • ₹65,000 - ₹210,000

Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science and Technology, commonly known as “Vel Tech Institution” is one of the leading Private Deemed University in india. The Institution is located in Chennai in the state of Tamil Nadu.

The Admission to Vel Tech Institution happens through two mechanisms i.e. through the entrance test or direct admission process. VTUEEE is the code for the entrance test. Candidates are able to register online or offline and review centers are spread throughout the world. Simple entry to college happens by locking off the seat by paying the premium. The applicant will be expected to fill out the specifics and select the course.

Vel Tech University Courses:

Courses : Vel Tech University academics is structured around Course Gradution listed below-
1. Bachelor of Arts + Bachelor of Law
2. Bachelor of Business Administration
3. Bachelor of Commerce
4. Bachelor of Commerce+Bachelor of Law
5. Bachelor of Science Honours
6. Bachelor of Technology
7. Master of Arts
8. Master of Business Administration

Accreditation : Vel Tech University was Accredited with Grade A & Accredited with Grade A NAAC.

Admission Process & Eligibility :

Vel Tech Institution offers admission in various courses such as BBA, MCA, BCA, B.Sc, B.Tech, MBA, M.Tech, etc. Vel Tech Admissions are based on the marks scored by students in 10+2. The admission to the Vel Tech Institution.

Before applying for the admission, candidates need to check the eligiblity criteria for each course. only if the applicant fullfil the eligiblity criteia then only the admission shall be granted.

B.Tech-Candidates should have completed Higher Secondary Exam (10+2) from a recognized board & Candidates should have to qualify VTUEEE which is conducted by the university itself

B.Sc-Candidates must have passed (10+2) exam from the recognized board.

B.Com-Candidates must have passed (10+2) exam with required percentage from the recognized board.

MBA-Candidates need to have a Bachelor's degree in any stream from a recognized university with a minimum of 60% marks. Also, a valid score of CAT/ MAT/ XAT/ GMAT/ TANCET/ CMAT is required for selection.

BA LLB [Hons]/ B.Com LLB-Candidates must have passed (10+2) exam from the recognized board.

BBA-Candidates must have passed (10+2) exam from the recognized board.

M.Tech-A graduation degree(B.Tech) & valid GATE score is required for admission in M.tech course.

M.Sc-Candidates need to have a bachelor’s degree in relevant subject.
