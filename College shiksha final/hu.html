Admission Process & Eligibility :

Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS), Tamil Nadu offers degree, diploma, integrated and industry collaborated programmes to candidates at UG, PG and doctorate (PhD) level. The popular courses of this University correspond to Engineering and Management streams. The University stood at 4th rank in Chennai, 18th in South India and 30th in all India among private Engineering institutions as per NIRF 2019.

HITS BTech admission is based on candidate's rank in HITSEEE.

BArch aspirants are required to possess a valid NATA score.

Admission to BA, BSc, BCom and BBA are accepted on the basis of merit.

BDes admission is based on a design aptitude test followed by an int erview.

For PG programmes, candidates are shortlisted based on their performance in the entrance exam conducted by the University, followed by GDPI.
