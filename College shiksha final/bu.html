Bharath University

  • Bharath University - Tamil Nadu

  • Tamil Nadu

  • ₹70,000 - ₹215,000

Bharath Institute of Science and Technology (BIST), Tamil Nadu, the first self-financing Engineering College was established by renowned academic visionary Dr.S.Jagathrakshakan under Sri Lakshmi Ammal Educational Trust in the year 1984. The trust then established Sree Balaji Dental College and Hospital in 1989 which was recognized as a Deemed to be University by MHRD in July 2002, under section 3 of the UGC Act 1956 under a new trust Bharath Institute of Higher Education and Research (BIHER) which was established in 1995. In January 2003, BIST was brought under the ambit of BIHER. Later, Sree Balaji College of Nursing, Sree Balaji College of Physiotherapy, Sree Balaji Medical College and Hospital (SBMCH), Sri Lakshmi Narayana Institute of Medical Sciences (SLIMS) were brought under the ambit of BIHER. BIHER provides high-quality education, multi-stream and multi-cultural to nearly 10000 students around the globe. The university is spread over a wide area of 305 acres of land with 1.3 crore sq.ft buildings located in 6 campuses both in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry with the best infrastructure facilities and well-qualified faculty. The university offers various Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Doctoral programs in multiple streams.

Admission Process & Eligibility :

Bharat Institute of Higher Education and Research (BIHER) was established in 1984. The institute offers courses at UG and PG level in various specializations. It also offers Ph.D., M.Phil, and MS by Research.

For B.Tech candidates must appear for the BEEE and admission is based on the rank secured.

B. Arch aspirants must pass the NATA examination.

For other UG courses B.Com, B.Sc, BBA, BA, BCA, etc admission is based on merit i.e. marks secured in the qualifying examination.

B.Sc Agriculture requires applicants to paper for the BEEE.

Admission to MCA, M.Tech, and MBA is based on the BEEE rank.

Admission to the Ph.D. and M.Phil program is done through a written test followed by an interview.

Placements :

BIHER has a very active Training and Placement Cell which works throughout the year to fulfil the requirements of students as well as industries. TPC organizes various workshops, guest lectures and seminars to improve the skills of students. Every year students get placed at top reputed organizations with handsome packages. The average salary offered to 2018-2019 batch was 3 LPA and maximum salary package was 15 LPA.
