The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. University is a government university, which was established in 1980 to deal exclusively with medical education and research at Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Doctoral level. The Tamil Nadu Medical University Act, 1987 received the assent of the President of India on 24th September 1987. By Act No. 39, the previous Act was amended that it might be called "The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R.University, Chennai, Act, 1987.

Dr. M.G.R.University Courses:

Courses : The university offers graduation, postgraduate and doctorate programs under each course. Super speciality courses and distance education programmes are also offered by the college.

It offers branches like Medical, Dental, Indian Medicine and Homeopathy, Allied Health Science, and the related programmes are MBBS, MD, DCP, DPH, DM, M. Ch; MDS, Diploma IN BDS; BSMS, MD, BUMS, BHMS, BNYS, M. Pharma, B. Pharma, B. SC, PB. B. SC, BPT, BOT, M. SC, M. Phil.There are many specializations under each course. Some of the important courses are mentioned below:
1. MBBS-10+2 (PCB)
2. BPT-10+2 (PCB)
3. B.D.S-NEET UG
4. M. Sc (Nursing)-B. Sc Nursing
5. M.P.T-B.P.T
6. M.S-MBBS
7. M.Ch.-MD or MS

Accreditation : MGR Universty was Accredited with Grade A & Accredited with Grade A NAAC.

Admission Process & Eligibility :

The Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University (TNMGRMU), a government university in Tamil Nadu. The university has different departments and offers a number of course at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral level.

At UG level MGR medical University offers MBBS and BDS degrees. A candidate needs to complete 10+2 for being eligible to these courses. The selection for these courses is done on the basis of performance in the entrance test conducted by the University. Interested candidates can register for these courses online. The registration process will begin from January 04, 2020.

Placements :

The Placement and Learning Cell of Dr. M.G.R University was set up with the primary purpose of encouraging the student population to position them in the industries according to their talents and abilities. Throughout the course of twenty-five years, the Placement Cell has experienced exponential growth and development. Students at the University work not only with India, but also abroad.
MGR has an excellent track record in placement not only in leading software companies like TCS, IBM, Infosys, Accenture, HCL, Caritor, Polaris, Wipro, L&T Infotech, Satyam etc but also in major Industries as can be seen from the records of students placed.

Numerous prestigious companies recruit Dr. MGR University students, including SBI LIFE INSURANCE, Virtusa, Axis Bank, IBM, TCS, Accenture, Wipro, HCL Technologies, Caritor India Pvt. Ltd., polaris, Cognizant, Wipro GE Healthcare.
