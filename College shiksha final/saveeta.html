SIMATS, Chennai was established in 2001 and has been awarded an A grade by the NAAC (UGC). SIMATS offers a comprehensive list of academic streams to students at UG, PG and super speciality levels (both in STEM subjects and humanities). The institute is famous all over the country for offering quality education in a variety of departments such as Medicine, Engineering, Law, Management, Pharmacy, and Architecture amongst others.

SIMATS has carved a niche for itself as a renowned institution for engineering education and research and is ranked 124 among all the IITs, central, state and private colleges and universities.

Admission Process & Eligibility :

Saveetha University, Chennai offers 110 UG and PG programmes in 11 academic departments covering diverse fields of education such as Engineering, Architecture, Management, Law, Medical, Nursing, Dental, Optometry, Physiotherapy, Allied Health Sciences and Pharmacy. A student-to-faculty ratio of 6:1 is maintained by the University, helping students to cultivate closer relationships with lecturers and having quicker access to feedback. SIMATS admission is purely provisional and is always subject to confirmation from Anna University / Directorate of Technical Education, Chennai.

For admissions to engineering courses, candidates must have passed Class 12 in PCM stream.

For BE/BTech, cut-off is based on last two years' Single Window Counseling allotted by Anna University via Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA)

Candidates must apply for NEET UG 2020 conducted by NTA for admission to MBBS and BDS programme.

Admission to BSc (Medical), BPT and BOT is strictly based on the scores obtained in the entrance exam conducted by Saveetha University
