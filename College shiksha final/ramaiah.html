Ramaiah Institute of Technology Located in Bengaluru, Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT) is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), which is headquartered in Belgaum. Set up in the year 1962, this autonomous private engineering college has been offering postgraduate and undergraduate programmes since August 2007. Although, VTU awards degrees, the college is free to frame its curricula and schemes of study. The college offers various undergraduate programmes in diverse streams like civil engineering, mechanical engineering, chemical engineering, biotechnology, and medical electronics. It also offers MCA and MTech in different streams, in addition to providing research programmes in various departments that lead to PhD/MSc (Engineering).

Admission Process & Eligibility :

Ramaiah Institute of Technology Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with Chemistry/ Biotechnology/ Biology/ Computer Science/ Electronics as optional subject and have obtained 45% marks (40% for SC/ST/Cat-1 and OBC candidates).

Placements :

Over the years, the college has seen a 95% placement record for the total batch size available in the college.Some of the leading names that are participating in the final placement session of Ramaiah Institute of Technology include Accenture, Wipro, Infotech, Infosys, 99acres.com, Healthmantra, ABB, Hindustan Unilever Ltd, Qualcomm, Adobe, HP (Core), Radio Mirchi, HP (IT) among various others.the students of this college are given coaching to acquaint them with the current corporate requirements to improve their personality and development CV writing, interview and GD skills. All of these soft skills training is mandatory. Also, prominent and experienced personalities are called at the campus for guest lectures and to conduct seminars around industry advancements.
